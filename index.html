Who We Are

Building Industry Leaders

Bierbower Enterprises, LLC is a holding company that unites industry-leading brands in the marine and fabric products sectors. From our headquarters in Cumming, Georgia, we drive innovation across multiple verticals, empowering our family of brands to deliver exceptional products to customers worldwide.

Our portfolio spans marine accessories, fishing equipment, wakeboard towers, and awning solutions. Each brand maintains its unique identity while benefiting from our shared commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
